Frequently asked question
What does "anonymity" mean?

You're able to ask and get answers to three, five and unlimited question monthly on the basic, premium and pro plan respectively. Being anonymous is choosing to not have your name shown when your questions are answered in the community. This feature not only helps you overcome the fear of asking deep, weird, touchy and controversial questions but it also allows for a lot f valuable self expression which otherwise won't exist. This feature is only available for the premium and pro subscription

Why are my questions posted and are seen by the community ?

Questions are posted as they afford not only the questioner insights but also enlightens other members of the community. This allows for valuable contributions, support and makes yor overal experience more fun and fullfilling. However, if you do not want your questions seen by the community, you can use the "Schedule consultation" feature where we tend to your issues on an even more personal level.

What are premium stories

Premium stories are improved stories with clearer focus, they're often stories that emody businesses, sales and lifestyle themes.

Are there free stories?

No, reading on Faaiz requires subscription. Your subscription unlocks stories and other resources we make available to suit your needs. This is designed to support our writers, team of experts, therapists and consultants. The subscription allows for an enhanced reading experience while aiding the sustainance of a home for our global community.

Can i upgrade my plan?

You can upgrade your plan at any time. If you choose to upgrade in the middle of a billing period, your account will be credited a pro-rated amount for the time remaining on your existing subscription and you will be charged for the upgrade with the credit applied

Do i get discount on apparels?

Being a premium and pro user entitles you to a wide range of benefits, this includes having access to discounted member prices. So, yes, you get discounts on apparels.
